Let's set up your business first. Add your company name, address, contact details, and logo — they'll appear on every invoice and report you send.

You can change any of this later in your Business Profile.

Use logo as app icon

Your logo is now the browser tab / bookmark icon. Reload the page once if the tab icon hasn't changed yet.

To update the iPhone Home Screen icon: iOS locks the icon in when you add the app, so an app that's already on your Home Screen keeps the old icon until you re-add it:

  1. Touch and hold the Bookkeeper icon → Remove App → Delete from Home Screen.
  2. Open the app's web address in Safari.
  3. Tap Share → Add to Home Screen.

The re-added icon will use your logo. (Your data stays safe — it lives in your account, not on the Home Screen.)

Position your logo

Drag to move, pinch or use the slider to zoom. The square is exactly what shows on invoices, reports, the home screen and the app icon.
