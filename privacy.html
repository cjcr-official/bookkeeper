Privacy Policy

Bookkeeper — operated by Case Johnston Computer Repair, LLC
Effective July 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Case Johnston Computer Repair, LLC (“we,” “us,” “our”) handles information in the Bookkeeper application (“the App”), a bookkeeping tool for managing invoices, customers, expenses, accounts, mileage, and related financial records. We keep this simple and collect only what the App needs to work for you.

Information we collect

  • Account information — the email address you use to sign in, and any business details you enter (company name, address, phone, logo).
  • Data you enter — the financial records you create in the App: invoices, customers, expenses, payments, accounts, mileage, jobs, and notes.
  • Bank data via Plaid — if you connect a bank account, we use Plaid to retrieve your account transactions so the App can reconcile them against your records. This data is retrieved live for reconciliation and is not retained by the App. Your bank login credentials are entered directly into Plaid and are never seen or stored by us.

How we use your information

We use your information solely to provide the App’s features to you: storing and displaying your records, generating invoices and reports, sending reminders you enable, and reconciling bank activity. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

Bank connections and Plaid

Bank connections are powered by Plaid Inc. When you link a bank, Plaid securely collects your credentials and returns an access token that lets us fetch your transactions. That access token is stored server-side only and is never exposed to your browser or device. Plaid’s handling of your information is governed by the Plaid End User Privacy Policy.

How we store and protect your information

  • Your data is stored in Supabase (hosted Postgres), encrypted at rest, and is isolated to your account using row-level security so no other user can access it.
  • All traffic between the App and our servers is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or better (HTTPS).
  • Bank access tokens and application secrets are held server-side only and are never sent to the browser.
  • You can protect access with an on-device passcode, Face ID / passkey, and optional account-level two-factor authentication.

Who we share it with

We share information only with the service providers that run the App on our behalf:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage.
  • Cloudflare — application hosting and delivery.
  • Plaid — bank account connections (only if you link a bank).

We may also disclose information if required by law. We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your records for as long as your account is active so the App remains useful to you. You can delete individual records at any time within the App, and you can disconnect a linked bank at any time (which also removes the stored bank access token).

You can permanently delete your entire account yourself from Settings › Account › Delete account (you'll be asked to type “DELETE” to confirm). This immediately and irreversibly removes your login and all associated data — invoices, customers, expenses, accounts, mileage, jobs, uploaded receipts, and any linked bank connections. You may also contact us at the email below and we will delete your account on your behalf.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws.

Children’s privacy

The App is intended for business use by adults and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Effective” date above and post the updated policy at this address.
